MondiAfrica

Founded in 2016 and based in Kanengo, mondi africa is a growing leader in the supply of high-quality agricultural products. We provide a wide range of essential commodities, including Ground Nuts, Soya Beans, Maize, Kilombero Rice, and Cow Peas. We pride ourselves on serving schools, private companies, and NGOs across Africa with efficiency and deep industry expertise.


Our commitment goes beyond just supplying products; we focus on anticipating our customers' needs and delivering nutritional, top-tier goods with exceptional service. Since our establishment, we have seen remarkable growth, with our annual turnover increasing by 65.3%. Starting with sales of MK9.3 million in 2016, we reached MK15.4 million in 2017, and successfully hit the MK20 million mark in the 2018 financial year.

👁️

Our Vision

To be the preferred supplier leader of quality and nutritional cereals and agro products to both local as well as international markets.
