Who We Are

Trusted Supplier of Quality Agro Commodities

MondiAfrica is a steadily growing agro-business company founded in 2016 and based in Kanengo, Lilongwe. We specialize in supplying premium cereals and diversified agro-products including Kilombero Rice, Groundnuts, Non-GMO Maize, Soya Beans and more.

We proudly serve schools, NGOs, corporate institutions and businesses across Malawi, providing reliable supply, competitive pricing and exceptional quality standards.
